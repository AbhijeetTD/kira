Features

Everything your SRE team needs,
handled automatically.

Intelligent Detection

Continuously monitors pods, deployments, and services. Detects CrashLoopBackOff, OOMKilled, ImagePullErrors, and more.

Multi-Agent War Room

Spins up specialized AI agents — SRE, App, Security, and Cost — that collaborate to find root cause in seconds.

Autonomous Remediation

Applies fixes automatically when confidence is high. Asks for approval on complex changes with full command preview.

Auto-Postmortems

Generates detailed incident postmortems with root cause, timeline, and prevention steps. Exports to Jira automatically.

Jira Integration

Creates, updates, and closes Jira tickets automatically throughout the incident lifecycle. Zero manual work.

Sherlock AI Chat

Chat directly with the cluster. Ask "Why did production go down yesterday?" and get instant, context-aware answers.
